Insurance calculators

These 18 tools give you an illustrative estimate — not a bindable quote — so you can walk into any insurance conversation with a sense of what you might need and what it might cost. Every result is clearly labeled as an estimate, with the methodology behind it disclosed alongside your number.

  • Auto Insurance Premium Estimator

    Estimates a monthly auto insurance premium range from inputs like ZIP code, vehicle, driving record, and desired coverage level.

    Related: Auto Insurance

  • Home Insurance Coverage Calculator

    Estimates a recommended dwelling coverage amount and premium range based on your home's size, construction, and location.

    Related: Homeowners Insurance

  • Life Insurance Needs Calculator

    Uses the DIME method (Debt, Income, Mortgage, Education) to estimate how much life insurance coverage a household may need.

    Related: Term Life Insurance

  • Term vs. Whole Life Comparison Calculator

    Compares illustrative term life and whole life premiums side by side, including a non-guaranteed whole-life cash-value projection.

    Related: Term Life Insurance

  • ACA Health Insurance Subsidy Estimator

    Estimates a household's potential ACA marketplace premium tax credit using the official federal subsidy formula and current-year figures.

    Related: Health Insurance

  • HSA/FSA Savings Calculator

    Estimates potential tax savings from contributing to an HSA or FSA, checked against current-year IRS contribution limits.

    Related: Health Insurance

  • Disability Insurance Needs Calculator

    Estimates the gap between your income-replacement target and any existing employer disability benefit, plus an illustrative premium for that gap.

    Related: Disability Insurance

  • Long-Term Care Cost Calculator

    Projects the illustrative cost of long-term care services in your state based on a cited cost-of-care survey and your assumptions.

    Related: Long-Term Care Insurance

  • Medicare Plan Cost Comparison Calculator

    Compares estimated total annual costs across Original Medicare with Medigap versus Medicare Advantage using CMS-sourced plan data for your county.

    Related: Medicare Supplement (Medigap)

  • Pet Insurance Cost Estimator

    Estimates a monthly pet insurance premium range based on your pet's species, breed, age, and desired coverage options.

    Related: Pet Insurance

  • Umbrella Insurance Coverage Calculator

    Recommends an illustrative umbrella liability limit based on net worth and risk factors, and flags if your underlying policy limits may be insufficient.

    Related: Umbrella Insurance

  • Renters Insurance Coverage Calculator

    Estimates recommended personal-property and liability coverage amounts, and a resulting premium range, for a renters insurance policy.

    Related: Renters Insurance

  • Flood Insurance Cost/Risk Calculator

    Looks up a property's FEMA flood zone and estimates an illustrative annual flood insurance premium range based on that zone and building details.

    Related: Flood Insurance

  • Workers' Comp Premium Estimator

    Estimates an annual workers' compensation premium range from your state, industry classification codes, and payroll.

    Related: Workers' Compensation Insurance

  • General Liability Insurance Calculator

    Estimates an annual general liability premium range based on your business's industry classification, revenue, and desired coverage limits.

    Related: Business/Commercial General Liability Insurance

  • Cyber Insurance Risk/Cost Calculator

    Estimates an annual cyber insurance premium range and an illustrative risk score based on your industry, data footprint, and security controls.

    Related: Cyber Insurance

  • Deductible vs. Premium Trade-Off Calculator

    Compares how raising or lowering your deductible could change your premium across auto, home, or renters policies, with an estimated break-even point.

    Related: Auto Insurance

  • Multi-Policy Bundling Savings Calculator

    Estimates a potential savings range from bundling multiple policies, such as auto and home, with the same carrier.

    Related: Homeowners Insurance
